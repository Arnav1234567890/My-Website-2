Our Mission

We envision to promote the Joy of Reading, especially in the underpriveledged communities. We envision a world wherein everyone enjoys the gift of education and Reading. We aim to continue sustainig our current libraries, and to set up more, in the distant future.

How It All Started

On the 17th of October, 2017, four friends came together to join and make The Nirupa Reading Foundation, dedicated to help provide books and education to underprivelegded communities, in the fond memory of their friend, Nirupa. It was our small way of keeping her alive in our memories by embarking on a journey to transmit her love for reading to more people in this world.

Our Progress

As of now, we have launched 3, successful and sustainable libraries, in the cities, Banglore and Gurgaon (India) respectively. We have recieved over 5000 books, dozens of volenteers, and have touched the lives of over 700 children.
